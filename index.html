About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Zurich Robotics & Perception Group led by Prof. Davide Scaramuzza.

My research interest lie in the intersection of neuroscience, machine learning and robotics. I currently focus on visual-motor coordination and decision-making in first-person view drone racing.
